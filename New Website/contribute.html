Would you like to help out the orchestra?

With a philanthropic gift of $200 or more (we leave that up to you), you can become an Orchestra Benifactor and be placed on our Benifactor roll for twelve months. This roll will be published in each concert program and on our website for twelve months. As a benifactor you will also recieve two complimentary tickets to our next concert, be placed on the mailing list for our newsletter and will be a guest at our annual Season's Lunch function. Please complete the details below, then print out and mail to PO Box 576 Townsville 4810. Make any cheques payable to North Queensland Ensembles Inc. You may also email us by completing the form and pay by direct debit to BSB 034 222 Acount no. 173922. Donations are tax deductable. For more information, contact Simon McConnel on 0418776961 or mcconnellgrey@optusnet.com.au You can also send us a message here. Your support of the Orchestra will be greately appreciated.
