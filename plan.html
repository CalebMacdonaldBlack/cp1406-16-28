Goals

Success Evaluation

Target Audience

1. Our primary target audience are younger people in their teens

2. How will we intentionally design our website for this target audience? Through research on usability if websites for teenagers we were able to determine that teenagers are incredibly social, love sharing content, use a smartphone regularly for browsing and can be very impatiant amung other things. We will intergrate facebook into our website to allow users to like and share content. Our website will be responsive and browsable on most devices and will be very clean and simple to use. Text heavy content will be hidden away until the user tries to look for it.
